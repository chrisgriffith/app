Fairy Lyly is a dynamic novel for iPad, suitable for children from 2 to 6 years old. 
The story is about a young girl, Lyly, who discovers to be a fairy, and her adventures in a wonderful world of magic.  
The flow of the novel and the tiny movements of the animations allow the enchantment of children and adults, without loosing the elements of the classic bedtime novel. Enjoy it!
