Nudge brings all of your healthy living together in one app, with one score. Connect Nudge with your favorite tracking apps like Moves, RunKeeper & MapMyFitness to see how your Nudge Factor stacks up against the rest.

Nudge provides a way to compare any quantified-selfer, analyzing your hydration, nutrition, activity & sleep habits in one place. Connect your apps, connect with friends & join the movement to create a single health score & a single feedback system for your healthy lifestyle.
