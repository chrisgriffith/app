Kids Math is a Smartphone game aimed at perfecting kids learning to recognize, write, and count math digits in a highly interactive fashion. It offers 3 major absorbing educational levels stages of Park, Forest and a Zoo to kids which further gives thorough experience of knowing about and writing digits, knowledge about geometric shapes and drawing them, animals and counting them. Each phase also consists of quiz & exercise corner where a child can review its gained knowledge.
